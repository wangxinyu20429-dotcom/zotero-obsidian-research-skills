# Idea quality gates

Zero candidates is a valid PASS when the record names failed gates, traceable blockers and the minimum evidence action needed to reopen review. Never create a candidate merely to satisfy a quantity target.

Apply these gates before recommending or writing an Idea as viable.

## Fatal gates

An Idea cannot be `建议优先开展` while any fatal gate remains:

- no testable scientific or engineering decision question;
- alleged gap supported only by “few studies found” or filenames;
- closest checked work already resolves the same question with no material new boundary;
- core conclusion depends on unavailable data with no credible acquisition route;
- no validation or falsification design;
- contribution is only workload, regional substitution, parameter tuning, or method accumulation;
- access, ethics, license, privacy, or engineering constraints make the study impermissible;
- scope cannot form one coherent paper argument.

Label unresolved fatal gates `待核验` and downgrade the recommendation.

## Scoring

Score 1–5 with a one-sentence evidence-based reason for each dimension:

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| scientific importance | marginal | relevant bounded problem | consequential general or decision problem |
| novelty | duplicate/renaming | meaningful extension | clearly differentiated contribution |
| data availability | core data absent | partial/obtainable | ready, documented, fit for scale |
| method feasibility | no testable route | feasible with work | executable and reproducible now |
| result interpretability | likely descriptive/ambiguous | bounded interpretation | discriminates mechanisms/decisions |
| validation conditions | none | partial/internal | independent or strong sensitivity design |
| publication potential | unclear outlet | realistic journal class | strong fit and contribution depth |
| team/project fit | outside capability | partial fit | direct leverage of data/project/task assets |

Do not use the total score to override a fatal gate. Keep missing evidence visible rather than awarding optimistic points.

## Recommendation

- `建议优先开展`: no unresolved fatal gate; question, evidence, data, contribution, and minimum validation align.
- `建议修改后开展`: valuable direction, but scope, gap verification, data, or method needs a defined repair.
- `建议作为附属分析`: useful evidence or module but insufficient for an independent paper.
- `暂不建议开展`: duplicate, infeasible, non-falsifiable, or unlikely to yield reliable contribution.

## Minimum validation gate

Specify the smallest analysis that can distinguish competing explanations or terminate a weak Idea. It must include:

- input data and sample;
- comparison/baseline;
- method;
- measurable output;
- success criterion;
- failure/termination criterion;
- expected time and dependencies when known.

Do not propose a full project as the minimum validation.

## Final audit

- Every major novelty statement names the checked prior-work boundary.
- Every evidence row displays the complete source name and a usable DOI/publisher/Zotero/Obsidian link; no conclusion cites only an ID or locator.
- Every data claim links to metadata or a verified file.
- Every proposed result answers a scientific question.
- Every main figure has one question and no duplicate purpose.
- Data, parameter, model-structure, spatial-scale, and extrapolation uncertainty are separated when applicable.
- Journal tiers are described without fabricated metrics; current scope or metrics are verified if stated.
- The final recommendation states the decisive evidence, decisive unknown, next action, and termination condition.
